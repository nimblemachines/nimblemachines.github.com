dipmicro electronics

dipmicro are a small, quirky, online electronic parts vendor based in Ontario, CANADA.

They carry a modest but useful selection of DIP integrated circuits (PICs, AVRs, some HC logic, common op-amps), leaded passives and semiconductiors (LEDs, bipolar and MOSFET transistors, diodes, voltage regulators), and a few surface-mount parts (SOIC ICs, surface-mount resistors and diodes, maybe others).

At the moment they are my go-to place for through-hole prototyping basics:

  • proto boards (good for DIP and SOIC parts)
  • LEDs (including very bright white ones) – these seem very cheap compared to elsewhere on the Web
  • crystals
  • leaded resistors
  • USB connectors (esp full-size B connectors)
  • header pins

They were the main source of parts for my JS16 USB board.
