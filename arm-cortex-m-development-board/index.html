ARM Cortex-M development board

Here is a short list of some very inexpensive Cortex-M boards that I know about:

  • FRDM-KL25Z – the first Freescale Freedom board
  • FRDM-KL46Z – another Freescale Freedom board
  • FRDM-K64F – yet another Freescale Freedom board, but for the Kinetis K series
  • Stellaris Launchpad
  • Tiva Launchpad
  • STM32F0 Discovery
  • STM32F4 Discovery
  • Teensy 3.0
